THE MANIFESTO

"Markets are not random. They are stories repeated in rhyme."

Our Mission

Regime Zero Capital was founded on a singular, radical premise: Human emotion is the greatest inefficiency in financial markets.

By removing the human trader from the loop and replacing them with a consensus of specialized Artificial Intelligences, we aim to achieve a level of objectivity, speed, and historical recall that is biologically impossible.

The "Zero" Philosophy

Zero Bias. Our models do not have egos. They do not hold losing positions out of pride.
Zero Latency. We process news the millisecond it hits the wire.
Zero Hallucination. Through our proprietary "Meta-Reviewer" architecture, we cross-verify every claim against hard data.

The Future

We are not just building a hedge fund. We are building the Market Operating System of the 21st century. A system that doesn't just predict the future, but understands the past better than any historian.
