### ðŸš€ Step 2: Regime Generation (The Creator)

**Prompt Template:**
> "You are a Market Historian. Define the 'Regime' of this market snapshot."
>
> **Output:**

> 4.  **Why Match**: 5 sentences on structural similarity.
> 5.  **Risks/Upside**: 3 each.
> 6.  **Style**: Academic/Insightful.

*Result*: A collection of 100+ unique, narrative-rich Regime Objects.
